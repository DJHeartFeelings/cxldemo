<td><br /><b>菜系及功效：</b>日本料理 <br /><b>生鱼片拉面的制作材料：</b><br /><b>主料：</b>蓝藻面100克(100g blue-green alagae noodles) 生鲜大麻哈鱼片100克(100g raw salmon slices) 白萝卜丝少许(shredded Chinese radish) 熟白芝麻少许 (cooked white sesame seeds as needed)(1) 柴鱼高汤6杯() (6C bonito soup broth) 酱油1大匙(1T soy sauce) 盐1/2茶匙(1/2t salt) (2) 绿芥末2大匙(2T wasabi paste) 酱油1大匙(1T soy sauce) 柴鱼高汤1大匙 (1T bonito soup broth) 熟白芝麻1/2茶匙(1/2t cooked white sesame seeds)<p align="center" id="cpTuPian"></p><br /><b>教您生鱼片拉面怎么做，如何做生鱼片拉面才好吃</b><br />1.将调味料(1)混合，煮开成煮面汁；调味料(2)拌匀成蘸汁备用。&nbsp;2.把蓝藻面放入沸水中煮约2分钟捞出，再放入煮面汁中煮熟后，捞出沥干即盛入器皿中，表面撒上熟芝麻。&nbsp;3.将白萝卜丝、大麻哈鱼鱼片放入另一器皿中，食用时和面分别蘸食蘸汁即可。&nbsp;1.Combine&nbsp;seasonings(1)&nbsp;well&nbsp;and&nbsp;bring&nbsp;to&nbsp;boil&nbsp;for&nbsp;cooking&nbsp;noodles.&nbsp;Combine&nbsp;seasoning&nbsp;(2)&nbsp;well&nbsp;to&nbsp;make&nbsp;dipping&nbsp;sauce.&nbsp;2.Cook&nbsp;noodles&nbsp;in&nbsp;boiling&nbsp;water&nbsp;for&nbsp;about&nbsp;2&nbsp;minutes&nbsp;and&nbsp;remove&nbsp;to&nbsp;seasoning&nbsp;(1),&nbsp;then&nbsp;cook&nbsp;until&nbsp;done&nbsp;and&nbsp;remove&nbsp;to&nbsp;drain.&nbsp;Then&nbsp;place&nbsp;on&nbsp;serving&nbsp;plate&nbsp;and&nbsp;sprinkle&nbsp;surface&nbsp;with&nbsp;cooked&nbsp;sesame&nbsp;seeds.&nbsp;3.Place&nbsp;shredded&nbsp;radish&nbsp;and&nbsp;salmon&nbsp;slices&nbsp;next&nbsp;to&nbsp;noodles,then&nbsp;serve&nbsp;dipping&nbsp;sauce&nbsp;on&nbsp;the&nbsp;side&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;dip.Serve.<br />&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<br />Tips：<br />这里是用生鲜大麻哈鱼片，您也可以选自己喜爱的鱼种来搭配。&nbsp;Serve&nbsp;any&nbsp;kind&nbsp;of&nbsp;raw&nbsp;fish&nbsp;instead&nbsp;of&nbsp;salmon&nbsp;if&nbsp;desired.</td>
